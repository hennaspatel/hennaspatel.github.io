Mobile Content:
Video Everywhere

Video is projected to outpace all other content forms in the upcoming years. Nonprofits with some budget, or volunteer know-how will benefit from developing video content plans that have emotional appeal and an authentic voice.
Here are three reasons why nonprofits of every size will need to include video in their repetoire of storytelling. 

  1. 82% -  Video traffic will be 82 percent of all consumer Internet traffic by 2021.  (Cisco Systems, 2017). 
  2. 80% - Forbes reports that by 2019 -- 80% of online marketing will be video content. And that mobile appetite for video is currently increasing 100% annually (Craig, 2018).
  3. 90% - 90% of consumers reported that videos helped them decide whether or not they would purchase a product. While this is an ecommerce statistic, it is one that nonprofits who are looking ahead shouldn't ignore. (Why video matters to marketing | infographic | WebpageFX.2018)
