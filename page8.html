Estonia an i-Voting Success Story

In 2000 Estonia became first country in the world to mandate internet access as a human right. The country now reports that close to 90% of citizens have internet access at home (statista.com, https://ec.europa.eu). Here's what that means for citizens.

Universal Digital ID

Almost every government service in Estonia is available online. Voting is no excpetion.  

Vote and Check

Votes cast from a secure home computer can be checked on mobile device. Multiple votes can be cast. The most recent vote or paper ballot counted as final.                                            

Since 2005 and growing

Per Estonia's goverment website, recent elections have reported 30% of voters are i-voters (e-estonia.com).
