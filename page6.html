Universal Digital ID

Almost every government service in Estonia is available online. Voting is no excpetion.  

Vote and Check

Votes can be cast from a secure computer and checked via personal mobile device. Multiple votes can be cast with the most recent vote or paper ballot counted as final.                                            

Since 2005 and growing

Recent elections have reported i-voting percentages making up 30% of the overall voter turnout.

Estonia i-Voting

Almost every government service in Estonia is available online. Voting is no excpetion. Accomplished through the country's digital ID for each citizen, votes can be cast from a secure computer and checked by the voter via mobile device. Recent elections have reported i-voting percentages making up 30% of the overall voter turnout.
